# QuickPrompt Privacy Policy ## Data Collection QuickPrompt does not collect, store, or transmit any user data outside of your browser. ## Local Storage The only data stored by QuickPrompt is: 1. Your preferred AI assistant choice (ChatGPT or Claude) 2. Your theme preference (light or dark mode) This information is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage API and is not accessible to the extension developer or any third parties. ## Third-Party Services When you use the extension to interact with AI assistants: - The extension opens tabs to official websites (chat.openai.com or claude.ai) - Any data you share with these services is subject to their respective privacy policies - QuickPrompt itself does not process, intercept, or store any of these interactions ## Permissions QuickPrompt requests only the minimum permissions needed for its functionality: - storage: To remember your preferences - contextMenus: To add right-click options - tabs: To open AI assistant websites - activeTab: To access the currently active tab when you explicitly use the extension - scripting: To help automatically submit queries to Claude - Host permission (claude.ai): To enable seamless integration with Claude ## Updates This privacy policy may be updated to reflect changes in the extension's functionality. Any significant changes will be communicated through the Chrome Web Store listing. ## Contact If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support channel.
